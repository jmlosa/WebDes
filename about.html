KNOW MORE ABOUT REI

Hi! I'm Jessa Mae Losa from Manila, Philippines. Now, you might be wondering who Rei is.
Rei is my cosplay alias/nickname. I go by that name in the cosplay community for security
purposes since I get to meet lots of people, aka strangers.

I have been cosplaying since February 2011. So far, I have cosplayed more than 40 characters
from video games and Japanese animes. My cosplay ranges from loli girls to shota boys,
most are young-looking characters. Our team are also doing some original concept shoots.

I am cosplaying for the purpose of relieving myself from stress with my daily life. I could also be
considered as a cosplay hobbyist, but a passionate one. lol I do my best to make my cosplay
as accurate as possible. From costume to makeup, I always make sure that everything
is almost on point.

Thanks for visiting my website!
